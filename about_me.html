About Me

Hello! My name is Shenhao Xie.

I'm a first-year graduate student at the University of Michigan. I'm studying UX design at the School of Information. I have a passion about understanding people's needs and satisfying those needs by design human experiences.

Me and UX design

I am a sensitive person and I care a lot about other people's feelings, which is one of the reasons that I found myself so interested in UX design.


The other main reason is my passion for art and design. Outside study, I enjoy drawing and playing guitar. I'm also a big fan of cooking. My mother taught me how to cook. She is a housewife and used to do all the housework. I knew how tiring it was to take care of the whole family for her and I wanted to help. I learned how to cook from her so I can help her with the housework. Now, I enjoy cooking and I always find it exciting to try new recipes and create new dishes.
